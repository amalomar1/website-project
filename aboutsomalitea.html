About Somali Tea

Somali tea is from Somalia, which is based in East Africa, Somali tea is an essential part of Somali beverages. Somali tea in Somali is called ‘shaax’ and it is a spiced tea with the option of having it with milk or without milk. In the Somali community, it is a tradition to have afternoon tea and in Somali it is called ‘casariyo’. Traditionally, the Somali tea is more on the sweeter side. We as Somali’s love to gather and talk, we love to catch up with friends and families and the best addition to that is with Somali tea. The spice in the Somali tea gives and aromatic taste and smell, there is always a side dish that goes perfectly Somali tea. An example of a savouring snack is ‘qurac’ or ‘bur’ which is a Somali beignet or like a sweet fried bread.
